We Must STOP Bullying

What is Bullying?

Bullying is the treatment toward another individual that is generally aggressive, repeated over time, and represents an imbalance of real or perceived power.

Forms of Bullying Verbal
  • Teasing
  • Name-calling
  • Taunting
  • Physical Threats
Social
  • Spreading rumors
  • Public embarassment
  • Excluding an individual
  • Cyberbullying
Physical
  • Hitting/Kicking
  • Spitting
  • Tripping/Pushing
  • Taking or breaking property
Affects of Bullying

Individuals who are bullied may have negative impacts in many aspects in life such as

  • Physical Health
  • Mental and Emotional Health
  • Academic Standing
  • Social Standing

These effects may cause trauma and affect those continuing into adulthood
